Privacy Policy – Tic Tac Toe Game

This Privacy Policy explains how the Tic Tac Toe game app handles user information. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are integrated.

Data sharing

Children’s privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. It does not collect or store any personal information from children or adults.

Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there are no risks of data breaches related to user information.
